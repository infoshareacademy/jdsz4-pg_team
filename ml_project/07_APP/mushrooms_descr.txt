Veils
Some mushrooms have membranes referred to as veils that cover and protect the immature mushroom 
or the immature lamellae. There are two types of veils.

The membrane protecting the immature lamellae is called the partial veil 
and the membrane covering the immature mushroom is called the universal veil. 
As the mushroom matures these membranes break, sometimes leaving remnants on the stipe or the pileus.
